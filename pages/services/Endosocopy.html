Endoscopy

Endoscopy is a vital diagnostic and therapeutic procedure within our surgical department, offering a window into the body's internal landscape with unparalleled precision. Employing a slender, flexible tube equipped with a camera and light source, this minimally invasive technique allows our expert medical team to visualize and examine various organs and structures, from the gastrointestinal tract to the respiratory system.
