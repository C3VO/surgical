Our Story

--

Founded in 1997, the Surgical Department embarked on a transformative journey that mirrored the evolution of medical science. The late '90s marked the integration of endoscopic procedures, allowing for non-invasive examinations and paving the way for more precise diagnostics.
As the new millennium dawned, the clinic embraced ultrasound examination technology, enhancing our diagnostic capabilities. The early 2000s witnessed a groundbreaking shift with the incorporation of laparoscopy, a minimally invasive technique that revolutionized surgical interventions, reducing recovery times and improving patient outcomes. In the following years, the Surgical Department became a hub for oncological surgery advancements. The integration of cutting-edge technologies and multidisciplinary approaches ensured comprehensive care for cancer patients. By the 2010s, the clinic was at the forefront of oncological surgery, offering state-of-the-art treatments and personalized care plans.
The 2020s brought about a synthesis of technologies, with advancements in genetic understanding and precision medicine shaping the landscape of surgical oncology. Today, in 2024, the department continues to thrive, offering a comprehensive range of surgical solutions that reflect the commitment to excellence established since its inception. The Surgical Department stands as a testament to innovation, combining expertise and advanced technologies for the optimal care of patients.
