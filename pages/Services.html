Services

Providing Healthcare You Need

Endoscopy

Endoscopy is a minimally invasive medical procedure that employs a flexible tube with a light and camera to visually examine internal organs. It aids in diagnosis, surveillance, and treatment planning.

Ultrasound examination

Ultrasound examination utilizes high-frequency sound waves to produce real-time images of internal structures. This non-invasive diagnostic tool is valuable for assessing organs, tissues, and fetal development in a safe and painl-ess manner.

Laparoscopy

Laparoscopy is a surgical technique involving small incisions and a camera-equipped tube (laparoscope) to visualize and operate on abdominal organs. This minimally invasive approach reduces recovery time and postoperative discomfort.

Oncological surgery

Oncological surgery within the surgical department focuses on skillful excision and treatment of cancerous growths. This specialized care integrates precision and advanced techniques to optimize patient outcomes in the fight against cancer.
