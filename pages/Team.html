Our Team

The surgical team at our hospital is a dedicated group of skilled professionals, including surgeons, nurses, and support staff, working seamlessly to provide exceptional and compassionate care. Their expertise ensures precision in surgical procedures, promoting patient well-being and recovery.

CHIEF OF THE DEPARTMENT

Jack Murphy

With an illustrious 20-year career, the Head of the Surgical Department has demonstrated exceptional proficiency, successfully performing over 5000 surgeries. His expertise spans Endoscopy, Ultrasound examination, Laparoscopy, and Oncological surgery, contributing significantly to our hospital's reputation for excellence. A trailblazer in surgical innovation, his leadership ensures the highest standards of patient care, fostering an environment of trust and continual advancement in surgical practices for optimal healthcare outcomes
